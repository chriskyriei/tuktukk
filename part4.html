A living paradox

stitched not in skin, but in stardust.
Bright as fire,
yet soft enough to warm without burning.
Steady as an ancient stone,
yet playful enough to dance with shadows.
You are contradiction’s masterpiece...
a balance of forces that shouldn’t coexist
and yet,Somehow,in ways no one can understand, they do, in you.
as if the universe conspired
to weave opposites into harmony.
Your strength is not the iron kind...cold,rigid and unbending,
it is the strength of oceans,
breaking against rocks,
retreating, returning,
still embracing every shore.
Yet even oceans drift,
and so you too wander away,
too far, too quiet..
but always returning
with tides swollen with feelings.
You are beauty,
not fragile or staged,
but the kind that arrives unannounced,
and hushes crowded rooms.
Your voice,a hymn no choir could imitate,
the sound of truth in a world scuplted of lies.
You are the world,
because your presence is gravity.
Life move toward you
the way stars gather around light
not by persuasion,
but by inevitability.
Words stumble in your shadow...
even the longest can't measure your reach.
Yet one day, you'll see it yourself,
a reflection lit with joy,
a future sewn with peace,
the proof of the happiness you’ve given.
You are moods and tempests,
a pendulum swinging,
a river dividing into restless paths.
Indecision is your chaos,
but chaos itself is aliveness,
and you live in ways
most only dream to.
In this world of masks and mirrors,
you are unfiltered breath.
In an age of glass and glitter,
you are flesh, pulse,laughter that refuses to be quiet,
tears that refuse to be hidden.
Raw. Rare. Real.
what’s special isn’t the date.
It’s that the world holds you in it,
and I get to know what life in the truest form looks like
